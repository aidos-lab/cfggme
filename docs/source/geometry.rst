scott.geometry.measures.forman
-------------------------------
The Forman curvature measure is designed to assess the curvature of a space based on combinatorial structures. It is often used in the context of discrete geometry and topology.

scott.geometry.measures.ollivier
---------------------------------
The Ollivier-Ricci curvature is a discrete version of Ricci curvature and can be used to analyze networks and graphs, capturing a form of geometric structure through metric spaces.

----------------------------------
